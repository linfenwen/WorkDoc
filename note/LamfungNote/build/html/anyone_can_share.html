Where to enable anyone can share¶

  1. [Participant can grab presenter role] and [End-to-End] options in superadmin

  3. webex.ini
  4. clientparam
  5. MeetingType
  6. SiteConfigExt & 0x20000

  1. SetEnableParticipantGrabPresenter

  1. CanMakeMePresenter

  2. MakeMePresenter

  3. QueryMakePresenter

  4. MakePresenterDirectly

    • CPfwServiceMgr::SetUserAsPresenter

Be make me presenter¶

  1. g_pMCServiceMgr->m_mcFeatureMgr.CanMakeMePresenter()
  2. && ((g_pMCServiceMgr->m_Info.dwBitFlag & BIT_FLAG_ANYONE_CAN_SHARE) != BIT_FLAG_ANYONE_CAN_SHARE_DISABLE)

Be Any one share¶

  1. g_pMCServiceMgr->m_mcFeatureMgr.CanMakeMePresenter()
  2. && ((g_pMCServiceMgr->m_Info.dwBitFlag & BIT_FLAG_ANYONE_CAN_SHARE) != BIT_FLAG_ANYONE_CAN_SHARE_DISABLE)
  3. && (CSmFeatureMgr::MP_MODE_ANYONE_CAN_SHARE)
